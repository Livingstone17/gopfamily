Word of wisdom
Gbemisola Adejamz April 13, 2020

The word wisdom reflected 222 times in the Old Testament, it was seen in Job, Psalms, Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, the Song of Solomon, the Book of Wisdom, and Sirach. " _For wisdom is a defence, and money is a defence: but the excellency of knowledge is, that wisdom giveth life to them that have it" (Ecclesiastes 7:12). Many things can lengthen your life, but only wisdom can save it.
